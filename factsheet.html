How It Works

Airshade's patented actuator uses the sun's heat to expand air — creating pressure that physically moves shading panels.

1

Air Expands with Heat

Sunlight heats air chambers in shading units

2

Pressure Rises

Expanding air inflates soft robotics actuator

3

Shading Units Move

Mechanical motion moves shading panels

Key Benefits

Zero Running Costs — No electricity, no cables, no ongoing energy costs

Plug & Play — No wiring, no control systems, works for retrofits and new builds

Modular & Scalable — From a single window to an entire building skin

Silent & Durable — No motors, no noise, reliable long-term operation

Future-Proof — EU EPBD compliant, designed to match facade lifecycles

Zero Carbon — Fully passive system with no emissions during operation
