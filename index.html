Patented Physical Response

Airshade's patented actuator uses the sun's heat to expand trapped air — creating pressure that physically moves shading panels.

1

Air Expands with Heat

Sunlight heats sealed air chambers inside the actuator

2

Pressure Rises

Expanding air creates mechanical force

3

Shading Units Move

Panels tilt to block direct sunlight

Smart Facades
Reinvented

Zero Running Costs

Powered entirely by the sun — no electricity, no cables, no ongoing energy costs. No motors or sensors means dramatically lower CapEx and near-zero OpEx over the entire building lifecycle.

Plug & Play

No wiring, no control systems, no infrastructure changes required. Airshade retrofits onto new and existing buildings alike — simply mount and it works from day one.

Silent & Durable

No motors means no noise — perfect for residential and office environments. Mechanical simplicity ensures decades of quiet, reliable operation without component failure.

Future-Proof

Fully compliant with the EU Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD). While electronics fail after 15 years, Airshade's passive system outlasts the building facade itself.
