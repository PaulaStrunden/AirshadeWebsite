Responsive Facades
Reinvented

Zero Running Costs

Powered entirely by the sun — no electricity, no cables, no ongoing energy costs. No motors or sensors means dramatically lower initial costs and near-zero operating costs over the entire building lifecycle.

Plug & Play

No wiring, no control systems, no infrastructure changes required. Airshade works for retrofits as well as new buildings.

Modular & Scalable

Individual units click together into any facade configuration — from a single window to an entire building skin. The modular system adapts to any geometry, scales effortlessly, and can be extended or reconfigured at any time.

Lab Real Life

Silent & Durable

No motors means no noise — perfect for office and study environments. Mechanical simplicity ensures long-lasting quiet, reliable operation without component failure.

Future-Proof

Fully compliant with the EU Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD). While electronics and PV fails after a few years, Airshade is designed to match façade lifecycles.
